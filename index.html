About Us

Bad Joy Productions specializes in the creation of short-form narrative films for web and new media, with a focus on the African American audience. We are located in Houston, TX.

Our primary goal is to grow our online audience by creating fresh, highly engaging stories with memorable characters and themes.

Please be sure to follow us on social media in order to stay up-to-date on our current and upcoming film productions, as well as to view our casting calls and production crew calls.

-Paul T. CEO/Executive Producer
